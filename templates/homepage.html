Marathon History

The concept of the marathon derives back from ancient Greece, symbolizing the legendary run of a Greek soldier from the Battle of Marathon to Athens to announce victory. Over centuries, this endurance run evolved into the marathon we know today, uniting people from all walks of life to test their limits, celebrate health and fitness, and foster community spirit.

The City Marathon embraces this rich history, offering participants a chance to be part of a storied tradition while experiencing the scenic beauty and cultural vibrancy of our city.
